Add Enterprise-Level “CEO Message” Section to About Page

Enhance the About Us page of a corporate Oil & Gas website (Fredviv Oil & Gas Limited) by adding a premium, enterprise-level “Message from the CEO” section.

The design must feel:

Corporate

Executive

Trustworthy

Clean

Authoritative

Minimal and refined

Use brand colors:

Deep Green (primary accent)

White (background)

Charcoal / Dark Gray (text)

Subtle light gray section separators

📌 Section Placement

Place this section:

After:

Company Overview

Mission & Vision

Before:

Core Values

Operational Strength

This should feel like a leadership bridge between vision and execution.

🏢 Section Layout (Desktop)

Create a two-column executive layout:

Left Column:

Professional CEO portrait

Portrait style:

Corporate headshot

Neutral background

Soft shadow

Slight rounded corners (not overly circular)

CEO Name (Bold)

Title: Chief Executive Officer

Optional: digital signature beneath name

Right Column:

Section Heading:

“A Message from Our CEO”

Short executive address (2–3 paragraphs)

Use elegant typography:

Slightly larger line height

Comfortable reading spacing

Include subtle quotation mark graphic in background (very light opacity)

Add thin green accent line under the heading

📱 Mobile Layout

Image on top

Name & title below image

Message text below

Maintain strong spacing and readability

Avoid crowding

🎨 Styling & Design Details

Background: White or very subtle light gray

Add soft card-style container with:

24–32px padding

Soft shadow

12–16px border radius

Maintain generous whitespace

Use visual hierarchy:

CEO Name: Strong, bold

Title: Smaller, muted gray

Body: Clean paragraph spacing

Do NOT make it flashy.
Do NOT overuse green.
Keep it executive and minimal.

🧠 Emotional Tone

The section should communicate:

Stability

Long-term vision

Leadership strength

Industry credibility

Confidence in operational excellence

It should feel comparable to:

Major energy corporations

Institutional-level companies

Investor-ready branding

🏁 Output Requirements

Desktop version

Mobile responsive version

Clean component structure

Reusable section component

Proper spacing system
